Ubud : les secrets d'une ile qui transforme les ames

Mai 2025 - Villa Mimpi Ubud

Il existe des endroits sur terre qui ne se visitent pas - ils se ressentent. Ubud, nichee au coeur des collines verdoyantes de Bali, est de ceux-la. Installe a la Villa Mimpi Ubud, vous serez au coeur de tout ce que cette ville a de plus precieux.

S'immerger dans les rizieres

Les terrasses de Tegallalang sont parmi les plus photographiees d'Asie, sculptees selon le systeme d'irrigation subak inscrit a l'UNESCO.

La spiritualite balinaise

Le Temple de Tirta Empul est une experience profondement emouvante. Les ceremonies balinaises se deroulent au quotidien.

Ubud n'est pas une destination. C'est une transformation.

Fruits tropicaux (mangue, papaye, dragon fruit), specialites balinaises (nasi goreng), boisson chaude et fleurs fraiches.

Ubud: secrets of an island that transforms souls

May 2025 - Villa Mimpi Ubud

There are places on earth that are not visited - they are felt. Ubud, nestled in the heart of Bali's verdant hills, is one of them. At Villa Mimpi Ubud, you will be at the heart of everything this town offers.

The sacred rice fields

The Tegallalang terraces are among the most photographed in Asia, sculpted using the UNESCO-inscribed subak irrigation system.

Balinese spirituality

The Tirta Empul Temple is a deeply moving experience. Balinese ceremonies take place daily.

Ubud is not a destination. It is a transformation.

The art of the floating breakfast at Villa Mimpi

May 2025 - Villa Mimpi Ubud

At sunrise, you slip into the infinity pool. On the water, a tray awaits - tropical fruits, Balinese pancakes, fresh juices.

What's included

Tropical fruits (mango, papaya, dragon fruit), Balinese specialties (nasi goreng), a hot drink and fresh flowers.

The beginning of a perfect day in Bali.
